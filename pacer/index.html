Pacer

Pacer is a running-tracking and social media app designed for runners: Connecting, tracking, and competing. Users can track their fitness via GPS within the app, or log their fitness manually, among other features. Pacer allows you to set workout goals and weekly commitments. That can include targets for the amount of time you spend training, the distance you run, or the number of calories you want to burn. What distinguishes Pacer from other fitness apps is its elegant user interface and “social fitness” platform. In addition to providing useful statistics and visualizations of exercise data. Pacer allows users to socialize, motivate, and compete via online and mobile apps. Individuals can use these features to improve their performance and gain new insights into their workout routines. One important feature to note is that although it runs in the background of your phone, the app has been optimised so that it doesn’t drain your battery life like a lot of similar apps. In addition to the mobile app, Pacer provides an API that allows 3rd party apps to integrate with their services. These services allow users to share their data on personal sites, provide enhanced analytical tools, and many other features.
